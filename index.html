What’s Ranza?


Ranza is looking to help you structure and sell option to purchase contracts for your clients in reference to their properties


In selling these contracts, you’ll gain a commission flow while helping your clients gain liquidity from the contract premium.

What’s REOP?


A real estate option agreement is a contract between two parties that states that the option holder has the exclusive right to buy the property at an agreed upon purchase price at any point before the option expires.


Key Variables

  • Option Premium

    - Amount we pay your client for the option


  • Strike Price

    - Amount we pay for the property by expiry


  • Expiration Date

    - Date of expiry
